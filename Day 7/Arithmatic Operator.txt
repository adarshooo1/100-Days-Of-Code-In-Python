Operators:-
Python have different type of operators for different operations.
To create a calculator we require arithmatic operators

Arithmatic Operators

___________________________________________
| OPERATORS  |  OPERATOR NAME  |  EXAMPLE |
-------------------------------------------
|    +       |    Addition     |  10 + 2  | :- 12
-------------------------------------------
|     -      |   Substracion   |  10 - 2  | :- 8
-------------------------------------------
|    *       | Multiplication  |  10 * 2  | :- 20
-------------------------------------------
|    /       |   Divisional    |  10 / 2  | :- 5.0
-------------------------------------------
|    %       |     Modulus     |  10 % 2  | :- 0(Reaminder 0[When Divide])
-------------------------------------------
|    //      |  Floor Division |  10 // 2 | :- 5
-------------------------------------------
|    **      |   Exponential   | 10 ** 2  | :- 100(10 power 2)
-------------------------------------------
