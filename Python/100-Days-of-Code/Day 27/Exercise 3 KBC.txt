KBC stands for "Kaun Banega Crorepati", which is the Indian
version of the international game show "Who Wants to Be 
a Millionaire?". The show is hosted by Amitabh Bachchan and
airs on Sony Entertainment Television in India. It is a
quiz-based game show in which participantsanswer a series of
multiple-choice questions to win prize money. The show has been
very popular in India and has had several seasons.
